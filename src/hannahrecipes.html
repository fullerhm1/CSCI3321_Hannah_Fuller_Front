#1: Macaroni and Cheese Pizza

Two of my favorite foods combined into one! A personal favorite of mine

  • 3 cups of any type of macaroni and cheese
  • 3 slices of American cheese
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • Pre-made pizza crust
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese of your choice
  • Optional: Food toppings
  1. Make 3 cups of macaroni and cheese. (Hint: One regular-sized box should be enough to make the necessary amount.)
  2. To make the macaroni more cheesy, add 3 slices of American cheese and 1/4 cup of milk; stir well.
  3. Place the pre-made pizza crust on a pizza pan; brush the crust with a small amount of olive oil.
  4. Spread the macaroni and cheese evenly over the crust.
  5. Add 1/2 cup of shredded cheese; you can choose whatever cheese flavors you want for this step. (Optional: You can also add toppings such as bacon or sliced hot dogs to add some flavor to the product.)
  6. Bake at 450 degrees (fahrenheit) for around 10 minutes. If after 10 minutes you find the pizza is not as golden as you would like, bake for an extra 1 to 2 minutes at a time until you are satisfied.
  7. Slice the pizza and serve!

#2: Funnel Cake

I used to eat these at fairs all the time, so I get a lot of nostalgia from this.

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 quart of vegetable oil (used for frying)
  • Optional: Sweets for toppings

NOTE: A deep fryer is required for this recipe.

  1. Measure out and add the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt to a bowl.
  2. Add milk, eggs, and vanilla to the mixture; stir until you have a smooth texture.
  3. Pour vegetable oil into the deep fryer at 365 degrees, then pour the batter in. Several methods of creating the funnel style can be found under the "Why is it called funnel cake?" section here.
  4. Fry for 2-3 minutes, making sure to flip over the funnel halfway through; dry on a paper towel.
  5. Sprinkle powdered sugar over the funnel. (Optional: You can also add toppings in place of sugar, such as caramel or strawberries and whipped cream.)
  6. Enjoy your treat!
